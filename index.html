Senior Project

An online WebGL game. Part of my senior project.

Having completed two games prior, Led a long way down and Ceremony, my team and I took up the challenge of bringing the senior thesis exhibition into the virtual realm due to the pandemic.

I was the only programmer on this, collaborating with artists and other students to ensure that their senior thesis project was represented in the game. The game can be described as a virtual showcase where players could walk around and enter houses to view student's work in a gallery-like setting.

Technical brief:

  • Apache webserver hosted on AWS EC2 Instance
  • In-game text-chat
  • Supports 20+ concurrent users
  • UI design and interaction
  • Unity Mirror Networking
